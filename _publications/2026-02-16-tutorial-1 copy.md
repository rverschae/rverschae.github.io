Our tutorial on ["Face and Gesture Recognition using Event-based Cameras"](https://rodrigo.verschae.org/fg2026/Event_FG_Tutorial) has been accepted at The 20th IEEE International Conference on Automatic Face and Gesture Recognition ([FG2026](https://fg2026.ieee-biometrics.org/)), the premier international forum for research in image and video-based face, gesture, and body movement recognition, that this year will take place in Kyoto, Japan.

The tutorial builds on a similar tutorial presented at FG2020, and this time it is being proposed and updated by Rodrigo Verschae, Daniel Acevedo, Nicolas Mastropasqua, and Ignacio Bugueno-Cordova.
