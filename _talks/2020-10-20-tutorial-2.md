The tutorial on "Introduction Face and Gesture Recognition using Event-based Cameras"" was presented at The 15th IEEE International Conference on Automatic Face and Gesture Recognition ([FG2020](https://www.palermo.edu/ingenieria/2020/noviembre/ieee-international-conference-automatic-face-gesture-recognition.html/)), which took place online due to the COVID19 pandemic.

Tutorial presented at FG2020, prepared with the support of Ignacio Bugueno-Cordova.
